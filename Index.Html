About Olumo Rock

Olumo Rock is a massive granite outcrop located in Abeokuta, Ogun State. For centuries it acted as a safe haven for the Egba people during 19th-century conflicts; today it stands as a cultural monument and tourist attraction offering caves, historical exhibits, and panoramic views.

Natural Fortress

Used as strategic shelter and lookout during tribal wars.

Cultural Hub

Home to shrines, festivals and the nearby Itoku Adire market.

Accessible

Stairs, pathways and elevator for visitors of all ages.

Quick Facts

Height: ~137 meters • Location: Ikija, Abeokuta

Facilities: Museum, gallery, craft shops, elevator, viewing deck

Plan Your Trip

Getting there: Abeokuta is accessible by road from Lagos (~2 hours). Local taxis and rideshares available. Ask at your hotel about organised tours.

What to bring: Comfortable shoes, water, sun protection, and a small jacket for windy summit days.
